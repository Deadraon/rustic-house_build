About Rustic House

A cafe with the soul of a wooden lodge and the ease of a modern lounge.

Rustic House blends warm wood, soft amber lighting, comfortable seating and a relaxed bar-side energy. It is designed for coffee dates, group celebrations, casual work breaks and late evening hangouts with great food, music and drinks.
